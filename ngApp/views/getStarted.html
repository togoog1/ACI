Help us get started on your project or venue Allen Commecial Industries manufactures affordable, quality cabinets at our facility in Monroe, WA. We recognize the responsibility that comes with being a large manufacturer, and we are committed to being a good corporate citizen, to the benefit of our employees, owners, and the community at large.

We are a full service company with a wide range of capabilities. Established in 1989. We provide full design, manufacture and Installation services. In addition, our installation fleet with crews are ready to deliver and install all of our products. We are currently located in a 20,000 sq. ft. facility on 2.5 acres centrally located in the Dallas Ft. Worth area. We have a full millwork facility including; 2 table saws Sliding panel saw Edge bander Joiner Planer Cold press Dual head 10' x 24' CNC router Our vinyl department incorporates a mimaki Cg-160 FX cutting plotter Mimaki JV3 Printer A full service paint booth Design is the limit!
